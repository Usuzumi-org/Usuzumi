功能Features

不是再做一个日历，而是减少安排本身的摩擦。Not another calendar, but less friction around planning itself.

Sked 的重点不是把所有事情塞进格子里，而是帮你看清时间、精力和优先级之间的关系。Sked is not about fitting everything into boxes. It helps you see the relationship between time, energy, and priority.

01

时间块Time blocks

用时间块组织一天，把会议、专注、零碎任务分开，不让所有事情挤在同一个列表里。Organize the day with blocks for meetings, focus work, and small tasks instead of flattening everything into one list.

02

轻量复盘Lightweight review

每天结束时记录完成度、延期原因和明天最重要的一件事，让计划形成闭环。Record progress, delays, and tomorrow's most important action so planning has a clear loop.

03

安静提醒Quiet alerts

提醒只在必要时出现，用低干扰的方式提示下一段安排，而不是打断当前工作。Notifications appear only when needed, quietly guiding the next block without interrupting current work.

界面Screens

三个核心界面，覆盖从计划到复盘的完整流程。Three core screens cover the full flow from planning to review.

Today

当天时间块、任务与下一步行动。Today's blocks, tasks, and next action.

Plan

快速把任务放进可用时间段。Quickly place tasks into available time slots.

Review

查看完成度与明日优先级。Review progress and tomorrow's priority.
